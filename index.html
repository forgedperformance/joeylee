Why no crunches or sit-ups?

Avoid crunches and sit-ups for now as 47° lumbar lordosis means traditional sit-ups increase disc load at L4/L5. Dead bug, bird dog, hollow body and Pallof press build identical anterior core strength while keeping the spine neutral.
